Transitioning to Cloud Computing

Steps to transitioning

  1. Identify the number of applications and data storage sytems that will be hosted
  2. Perform market analysis to understand service proviers and costs; choose one
  3. Complete an initial deployment to the cloud and complete regression tests to ensure all functionality available
  4. Notify your users that the switch to cloud computing is now available
